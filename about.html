About Africa Geoscience Metadata Platform

A unified hub for geospatial data, projects, and platforms across Africa.

Our Vision

Empowering Africa with accessible, high-quality geoscience data to support innovation, sustainability, and informed decision-making.

What We Do

  • Provide a comprehensive geospatial metadata repository.
  • Support ongoing and completed geospatial projects.
  • Connect users with geospatial analysis tools and platforms.
  • Enable data-driven decision-making** for policymakers, researchers, and businesses.

Why Choose AGMP?

We bridge the gap between data providers** and users, ensuring that reliable, organized, and insightful geospatial information is available for decision-making.

Our Collaborators

We work with a diverse range of partners, including:

  • Government Agencies (for infrastructure planning and policy-making).
  • Research Institutions & Universities** (for scientific studies and analysis).
  • Private Sector Companies (for geospatial applications in industry).
  • International Organizations (for global collaboration on geoscience initiatives).
